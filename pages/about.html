About Dhanu Sir

Dhanu Sir is a Career Growth Coach, Digital Coach, IT Coach, Life Coach, and an Entrepreneur. His orginal name is Budh Ram Gurung. Dhanu is his nickname.

He is currently serving as a Director of Training and Placement in Intern Sathi, a platform which provides skilled and certified interns.

He is passionate about teaching and has started this platform to share his knowledge to the needy IT Students and Professionals.

He has been teaching independently from 2019 and helping IT students in gaining skills needed for software development job.

Previously, he has worked as software developer ranging from startups to MNC and has around 11+ years of IT experience.

He had profound knowledge on software development and went through different roles from Quality Assurance, Full Stack Web Developer, DevOps Engineer and Team Lead.

He is an Open Source Advocate and believes that Open Source helps to become a Better Software Engineer.
