About Dhanu Sir

Dhanu Sir is an IT Trainer, Career Coach and an Entrepreneur.

His orginal name is Budh Ram Gurung. Dhanu is his nickname.

He is currently working as the Founder & CEO of Code To Earn(c2e) which aims to produce the best Software Engineering talents from Nepal.

He has also founded a group known as Gorkha Dev with the mission to save this world from Bad Softwares.

He has been teaching independently from 2019 and helping IT students in gaining skills needed for software development job.

Previously, he has worked as software developer ranging from startups to MNC and has around 12+ Years of IT experience.

In his last job, he was working as a Mentor and Director of Training at CloudYuga Technologies who is providing Cloud Native technology trainings.

He had profound knowledge on software development and went through different roles from Quality Assurance, Full Stack Web Developer, DevOps Engineer and Team Lead.

He is an Open-source enthusiasts and consider himself as the product of Open-source community.
